About

  • Life

    Andrew, who goes by Drew, was born in Warner Robins, GA. Around the age of 4, his family moved to Kansas City, where he was spend the next 14 years of his life. Drew attended Sumner Academy of Arts and Science, which is an invite-only private school in the center of Kansas City, designed to place “gifted” students in a challenging environment. After graduating, Drew attended the University of Tennessee, where he would spend 3.5 years, before eventually enlisting in the United States Air Force. Drew left the Air Force in 2014, moved to Seattle, and has been there ever since.

  • Education

    Drew has an undergraduate degree in Information Systems, where he primarily earned from the University of Tennessee, with the actual degree being conferred by Ashford University while he was in the Air Force.

    Drew also has an MBA from Saint Leo University, with a concentration in Marketing.

    Drew is currently completing his Master of Computer Science from Northeastern University, which he will complete the Summer of 2017.

  • Career

    Drew’s career started in the United States Air Force, where he was an IT Program Manager for 6 years.

    In 2014, he left the USAF to continue his career at Amazon Corp, in Seattle, WA. He’s held multiple roles at Amazon, including Technical Account Manager, Campaign Manager and Program Manager, which is his current role.
